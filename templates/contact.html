Our Story

Service Pro Community originated on a notepad from behind the counter of a family owned taco shop, and has since become recognized across multiple states with expanion into Europe right around the corner! For several months this company lived only on paper and in sketches, while everyone who was involved participated in pitch competitions, advertising campaigns, investment research and everything else that went into creating a physical tangible company. Along the journey it was decided that SPC would be an app for the average individual, and would not be designed with the intent of generating profit. As a result of being user oriented, the Service Pro Community has evolved into a network of dedicated and driven professionals that work consistently to find solutions for those in their community. We are a small independently owned company that will change the world by restructuring the gig economy to be more rewarding for every individual involved. SPC will create a global community of support and personal evolution/advancement that will be beneficial to both individual users or consumers, as well as business owners and larger corporations. "It was always about helping as many inndividuals as possible, but it didn't take me very long to figure out I can't help very many people. So, I started helping people help people." - SPC Founder Blake Granner.
